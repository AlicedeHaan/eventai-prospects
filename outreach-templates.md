## TEMPLATE BY PROSPECT TIER

### Tier 1 - High Priority (50+ employees, experiential focus)
- **Approach:** Personal, reference similar work
- **Customisation:** Mention specific recent events you've seen them do
- **Follow-up:** 3 touchpoints over 2 weeks

### Tier 2 - Medium Priority (20-50 employees)
- **Approach:** Value-first, quick win focus
- **Customisation:** Reference their location/city focus
- **Follow-up:** 2 touchpoints over 1 week

### Tier 3 - Lower Priority
- **Approach:** Generic, automated
- **Customisation:** Basic personalisation only
- **Follow-up:** 1 touchpoint only

## CLOSING TACTICS

### For Interested Prospects:
- "Great - let's schedule your free audit for [DATE/TIME]. Here's what I'll need from you beforehand: [list of current docs/timelines]"
- "After the audit, I'll send you a short report with my recommendations. No obligation - just want to prove the value."

### For Skeptics:
- "Totally understand - most people feel the same. Here's what I'd suggest: try our free audit on just ONE event. If you don't see value, no more contact from us."
- "We can also start small - maybe just the planning phase for your next event. See how it goes."

### For Price Objections:
- "Think of it this way - if our AI saves you 15 hours per event and you run 4 events/month, that's 60 hours. At £50/hour, that's £3,000/month in time savings."
- "We also offer a pay-per-event model if retainer doesn't work for you."

### For "Need to Talk to Team" Response:
- "Absolutely - would it be helpful if I joined a quick call with your team to walk them through? Or I could send over a one-pager for you to share with them."
- "When would be a good time to circle back? Next week?"
